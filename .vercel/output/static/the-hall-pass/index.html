THE HALL PASS

Short Film | 11 Minutes | Drama/Thriller | Colour

In a chance encounter, a ride-share driver struggling to navigate jealousy in his relationship, picks up his partner's celebrity hall pass as a passenger.

CAST
Marc Donato, Rebecca Kwan, Noah Zulfikar and Demi Oliver
